# Documents
Cookbook recipes for document approval, classification, retrieval, AI processing, and human review workflows in Orkes Conductor.

Use these recipes when workflows need to classify, retrieve, approve, or route documents through AI and human review steps.

Start with the document workflow closest to your use case, then adapt the AI model, vector database, approval, and notification steps.

If you are unsure where to begin, start with **Document Approval**, **Document Classification**, **Document Retrieval Workflow**. These pages cover the most common entry points for this section.
